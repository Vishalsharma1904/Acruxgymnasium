Our Story

Your Neighborhood Gym, Done Right.

Acrux Gym is a local gym in Vishnu Garden, Loni, Ghaziabad. We've been serving the neighborhood since 2019. We're not a franchise or a chain — we're your local gym, run by people who genuinely care about your progress. Whether you've never lifted before or you train 6 days a week, you'll find your place here.
